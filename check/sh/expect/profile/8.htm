Time format:
*
* For experts: Format string according to PHP-function strftime().
If in doubt, leave empty.
Some strftime() specifiers (may be combined):
%a
Abbreviation of weekday
%A
Weekday
%b
Abbreviation of month
%B
Month
%c
Date and time
%d
Day of month as 2-digit number
%e
Day of month with leading space for single digits
%H
Hour in 24-hour format
%I
Hour in 12-hour format
%m
Month as number
%p
"AM" or "PM"
%S
Second as 2-digit number
Additional specifiers:
%+
Day "Arrival" as offset from today.
